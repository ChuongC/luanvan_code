An edited photo of Yogi Adtyanath is shared as the visuals of him watching Pathaan - FACTLY

Publication Date:2023-02-06T07:48:26+00:00
A social media post shares a video clip of the UP CM Yogi Adityanath allegedly watching the movie ‘Pathaan’. This video surfaced on the internet after the release and success of the film ‘Pathaan’ at the box office. Let’s fact-check this claim through this article.
Claim: Visuals of Yogi Adityanath watching ‘Pathaan’.
Fact: The viral video is digitally created using a photo of Yogi Adityanath watching the FIFA world cup. Hence the claim made in the post is FALSE.
To learn more details about the viral clip, we ran a reverse image search on a few keyframes of the video using the InVID tool. This led us to a news article published by ABPlive on 18 December 2022.
Reportedly, CM Yogi shared a picture of him watching the FIFA World Cup final on his Twitter handle. You can watch this photo here. The viral video was created using the same photo by replacing the football match visuals on TV with a song from the movie Pathaan.
