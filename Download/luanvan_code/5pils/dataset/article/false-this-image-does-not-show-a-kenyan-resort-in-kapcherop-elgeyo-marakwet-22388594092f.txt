FALSE: This image does not show a Kenyan resort in Kapcherop, Elgeyo Marakwet | by PesaCheck | PesaCheck

A photo shared on Twitter and identified as a tourist resort in Kapcherop, Elgeyo Marakwet county of Kenya’s Rift Valley, is FALSE.
The author of the post claims that the Kipteper tourist resort rates are KSh 10,000 for couples.
An online search of the name ‘Kipteper tourist resort’ does not bring any results associated with Elgeyo Marakwet County.
A reverse image search shows the picture first appeared online in 2016, published on the Travel Bay website. Further Google searches show that the picture is of Mystique Santorini Hotel in Greece.
Searching the keyword ‘ Mystique Santorini Hotel’ we found more photos from different angles of the resort.
PesaCheck has looked into a tweet with an image claiming to be of a tourist resort located in Kapcherop, Elgeyo Marakwet county and finds it to be FALSE.
