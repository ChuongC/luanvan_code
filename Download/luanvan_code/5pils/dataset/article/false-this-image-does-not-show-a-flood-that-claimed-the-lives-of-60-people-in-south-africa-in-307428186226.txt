FALSE: This image does not show a flood that claimed the lives of 60 people in South Africa in April 2022 | by PesaCheck | PesaCheck

A Facebook post with an image claiming to show a flood in South Africa that claimed the lives of 60 people in April 2022 is FALSE.
The Amharic text accompanying the photo translates, “60 people died owing to a sudden flood in South Africa.”
An image that shows a man walking over a flood is attached to the post.
However, Google reverse image search results reveal that the image has been shared online since 2021.
For instance, the picture was shared on this Facebook account on 13 December 2021. Similarly, the photo was posted on this Twitter account on 13 December 2021.
The media reported a flood disaster in South Africa in April 2022, with the death toll reaching more than 300, as reported here and here.
PesaCheck has looked into an image shared on Facebook claiming to show a flood in South Africa that claimed the lives of 60 people in April 2022 and found it to be FALSE.
