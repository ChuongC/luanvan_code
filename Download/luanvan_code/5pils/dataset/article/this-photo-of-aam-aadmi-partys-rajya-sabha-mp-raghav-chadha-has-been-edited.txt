This photo of Aam Aadmi Party’s Rajya Sabha MP Raghav Chadha has been edited - FACTLY

Publication Date:2022-04-01T08:28:37+00:00
A photo through a post is being widely shared on social media claiming it to be the photo of Raghav Chadha, the Aam Aadmi Party member, and Rajya Sabha MP. Let us fact-check the claim made in the post.
Claim: Photo of AAP’s Rajya Sabha MP Raghav Chadha.
Fact: The photo of Raghav Chadha has been edited and make-up added. The photo of Aam Aadmi Party member and Rajya Sabha MP Raghav Chadha is from the Lakme Fashion Week 2022; he participated for his maternal uncle who is a fashion designer. Hence, the claim made in the post is MISLEADING.
When reverse image search is done on the photo, a similar photo of Raghav Chadha was found in a tweet. According to the tweet, the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) leader, Rajya Sabha MP Raghav Chadha was participating in Lakme Fashion Week 2022. Raghav Chadha said, according to his Instagram page, that he dressed for his maternal uncle who is a fashion designer.
Youngest Rajya Sabha MP, The Youth Icon, A Cool Dude @raghav_chadha walking the ramp at Lakme Fashion Week ❣️ #LakmeFashionWeek2022 pic.twitter.com/vRM43T3Hhf
When the viral photo is compared with the photo found in the tweet, the viral photo has been edited and make-up was added to it.
To sum it up, this photo of Aam Aadmi Party’s Rajya Sabha MP Raghav Chadha has been edited.
