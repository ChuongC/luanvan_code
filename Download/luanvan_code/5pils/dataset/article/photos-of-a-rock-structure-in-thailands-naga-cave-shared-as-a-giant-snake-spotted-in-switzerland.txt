Photos of a rock structure in Thailand’s Naga cave shared as a giant snake spotted in Switzerland - FACTLY

Publication Date:2023-03-16T09:23:37+00:00
A collage of images is being shared on social media claiming it as the pictures of a scary massive snake found in a mysterious cave in Switzerland. Let’s verify the claim made in the post.
Claim: Collage of images showing a massive snake spotted in Switzerland.
Fact: These images show a snake-shaped rock structure located in Naka or Naga cave in Phu Langka National Park in the Bueng Kan province in Thailand. These pictures do not show a real giant snake and have nothing to do with Switzerland. Hence, the claim made in the post is False.
On reverse image search of the photos shared in the post, similar images were found shared in a Facebook post made on 29 May 2020. This post described it as pictures of a giant snake-shaped structure in Naka cave in Thailand. When we searched for further sources using relevant keywords on the internet, similar images were found in articles published by ‘News 18’, ‘OTV’ and other news websites. All these news websites reported it as images of a rock structure in the shape of a giant snake and said it was in Naka or Naga cave in Thailand.
Naga cave is a famous tourist attraction of Phu Langka National Park located in the Bung Khong district in Bueng Kan province in Thailand. A few more details of this snake-shaped rock structure in Naka or Naga cave can be seen here, here and here.
To sum it up, pictures of a giant snake-shaped rock structure in Thailand’s Naga cave are shared as a giant snake spotted in Switzerland.
